About Zion Gospel Ministry

A peaceful and welcoming church family committed to grace, biblical truth, and Christ-centered fellowship.

Church History

Foundational Prayer Gatherings

Started as a small prayer fellowship with a burden for Gospel-centered revival in families and neighborhoods.

Church Planting and Discipleship

Expanded into regular worship services, Bible study groups, and leadership training for local ministry.

Digital Ministry Growth

Launched online resources to disciple believers globally through sermons, studies, and prayer care.

Statement of Faith

Core Beliefs

  • One God in Trinity: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • Authority of Scripture
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Christ
  • Resurrection and return of Jesus Christ

Church Life

  • Prayer, worship, discipleship, and mission
  • Baptism and the Lord's Supper
  • Power of the Holy Spirit for holy living
  • Unity, compassion, and Gospel witness
